Overview

WooCommerce Product Trade-In Plugin allows you to buy back items from your customers. This similar feature can be seen at uSell.com and gazelle.com. Easily allow users to trade-in their items by automatically creating shipping labels for them once they submit their data.

Features

  • Create products just as you would regularly.
  • Give pricing based on your set conditions.
  • Simple checkout form for users with ability to choose payment method.
  • Automatic creation of shipping labels.
  • Track and manage shipping labels.
  • Pay users once you receive their item

Requirements

This WooCommerce extension requires WordPress 3.6 or later and WooCommerce 2.1 or later and an easypost.com account.
